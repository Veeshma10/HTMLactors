RAJNIKANTH


Shivaji Rao Gaikwad[a][4] (born 12 December 1950), known professionally as Rajinikanth,[b] is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Tamil cinema.[6] In a career spanning over five decades, he has done 170 films[c] that includes films in Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and Malayalam. He is widely regarded to be one of the most successful and popular actors in the history of Indian cinema.
